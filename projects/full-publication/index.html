Biography

I am currently a PhD student at Wuhan University, supervised by Prof. Yanfei Zhong , Liangpei Zhang and Assoc. Prof. Xinyu Wang. My research interest is in Earth Vison Interpreting, especially anomaly detection and foundation model technique for remote sensing imagery. My research goal is to build insightful and practical technologies for efficient anomaly detection, protecting the environment and social security for human. My research taste likes zero-shot and open-world settings.
