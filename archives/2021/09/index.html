Research Interest

I work in the field of remote sensing image quality improvement, low-level vision, and deep learning theory.
Currently, I focus on the following research topics:

  • Combined data-driven with model-driven strategy for low-level vision.
  • Satellite video imagery super-resolution.
  • Time-series remote sensing product reconstruction.
